Shridhar Kulkarni

The Academy

Shridhar Kulkarni, founder of ASTABEL Academy, has over twenty five years of experience as an IT leader, educator, and certified college counselor. Living true to the guiding principle of the organization, Shridhar has been guiding students and families toward the best possible educational options, which he hopes takes them a step closer to a better life. Shridhar works collaboratively with students and parents to help them plan and organize their college planning work and provides tools and processes so they can balance their academic demands with the college planning activities. Professional expertise, customized assistance, a well-structured process with defined time lines, define Shridhar’s exceptional service model. With a proven track record of helping students with their undergraduate, graduate, and MBA aspirations, Shridhar is ready to help students to bring their best and get ready for the college of their dreams.

The Founder

Shridhar’s educational accomplishments include a Master’s degree from MIT’s Sloan School of Management, a Graduate Degree Certificate in College Counseling from UCLA, and a Graduate Certificate in Arts Administration from Boston University. Shridhar is a faculty at Boston University, UMass Lowell, and Embry-Riddle University, teaching courses for undergraduate, graduate, and MBA programs, in Computer Science and Management areas. He has attended the coveted Summer Institute on College counseling program at Harvard University and has developed a deep appreciation and knowledge about how Ivy League and very selective schools evaluate applicants. Shridhar regularly conducts workshops and seminars on college admission process and essay writing.

Our Comprehensive Service

We understand and empathize with you as you start your exciting journey, which at times may be stressful, in finding the right college. We partner with you to offer the much needed expertise and bring a sense of balance and control to the whole experience.

We engage with students with different service offerings depending on where they are in the college planning process.

ASTABEL Academy, true to its name, is there for students every step of the way in finding the best fit school to pursue their education. We work with students from public schools, charter schools, private schools, and international schools. We assist students with all levels of academic abilities. Our services are customized for each student. We provide full service for an unlimited number of applications.

Following is the list of services that are included in our comprehensive package, for unlimited number of colleges.

Early planning
Early planning for high school freshman and sophomores
Course selection
Course selection, including rigor
Financial aspects
Financial aspects, including FAFSA, CSS Profile, and Scholarships
Standardized testing
Standardized testing recommendation and referrals, including SAT, ACT, SAT Subject tests, TOEFL and/or IELTS for International Students.
Summer Programs and Activities
Summer programs, based on field of interest and career aspirations. Extracurricular activities, Leadership roles, Research options
College search and selection
College search and selection, based on interested major, academic fit, and social compatibility. College visit plan and preparing for interviews. Review of acceptances/wait lists and assistance with communication and decision
Essays
Brainstorming ideas and reviews of drafts for Common App essay, Personal insight questions for UC, Coalition Application essay, and Supplemental essays

Student Login

Students who are availing our services can login to our web based tool that provides comprehensive application management process. The tool allows the students to communicate with their designated consultant through messages, securely upload their documents, update their standardized test results, research colleges based on identified criteria, manage the entire application process with visibility into college specific requirements, deadlines, and results.
